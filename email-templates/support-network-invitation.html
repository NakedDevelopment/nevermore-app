Support made for the people who care

While portions of the app are designed for individuals in recovery, Nevermore also includes support-focused audio content created specifically for trusted friends, family members, and loved ones.

Inside the Temptations audio library, you'll find a "Support" mode that explains:

  • what your loved one may be experiencing internally,
  • why certain struggles or behaviors happen,
  • and how to better support them throughout recovery.

This experience is designed to help you better understand the emotional and psychological side of recovery — not just for them, but for the people walking beside them as well.
